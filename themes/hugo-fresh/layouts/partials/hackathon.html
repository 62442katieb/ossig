A hackathon? For who? At the OHBM hackathon members of the community gather to work collaboratively on common projects.
Hackathons differ from typical academic conference in that because attendees can actively take part in the program and co-learn from each other will be actively trying to create something rather than passively attend. Although many hackathon projects might involve coding, it is not a requirement and many projects can be done without coding skills.
The event also features a TrainTrack in which tutorials on open science practices are provided.
